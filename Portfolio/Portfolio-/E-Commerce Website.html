Project: E-Commerce Website

Project Overview

An ecommerce platform is a software application that allows us to create, manage, and run our online store. It provides the essential features and functionalities for our ecommerce website, such as a product catalog, shopping cart, checkout, payment processing, order management, and more.

Project Details

Provide detailed information about each product, including images, descriptions, and prices.

Shopping Cart: Allow users to add items to a shopping cart, view their cart, and proceed to checkout.

Order Management: Enable users to view order history, track current orders, and manage

Development Process

*Shoppers visit our E-Tech website and browse products using a variety of devices, such as smartphones, laptops, Camera, headset, and power bank and Mini Ac.

*Customers typically add the products they want to buy to a virtual shopping cart. The shopping cart keeps track of selected items, quantities, and total costs.

*When customers are ready to purchase, they proceed to the checkout page. They provide shipping information, select a payment method, and review their order before finalizing the purchase.

*The seller receives a notification of the order

*The vendor then prepares the products for shipping to the customer

Technical Details

  • Tech Stack: Html/css,Javascript
  • Key Features: placement of products online, secure payment systems, and fullfillment of orders.
  • Technical Decisions:This website allows visitors to find their product, add them to their “cart,” and securely enter their payment information to complete their purchase
